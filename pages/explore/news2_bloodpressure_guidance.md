The NEWS2 blood pressure observation reading uses the [CareConnect-BloodPressure-Observation-1](https://fhir.hl7.org.uk/STU3/StructureDefinition/CareConnect-BloodPressure-Observation-1).

Only the systolic part of the blood pressure observation is used in the NEWS2 score, but systems should send both the systolic and diastolic blood pressure components.

# Guidance for Populating a NEWS2 Blood Pressure Observation #

When an Observation element has guidance "Not needed for NEWS2", this means that systems MAY populate the element (if appropriate) in the NEWS2 Observation.
